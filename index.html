Sharky and Fishton were the bestest of friendly friends. But everyday Fishton had to go to school and leave Sharky so Sharky was sad.

Sharky kept being sad when Fishton left him, so as he wept he came up with an idea.

Then next day as Fishton left home he came to the school but no fish was there. And as he checked the cameras, he saw it all. Sharky had ate all the school fish!

Just as Fishton was going to report this crime to the fishlice (fish police), Sharky swam at him and chomped him up. Sharky did not want to go back to oceanrison (ocean prison).

And that is the moment Sharky realised he killed his best friend all because he dicided to take drugs to suppress his sadness.

The End
